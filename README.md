# Supply Chain Sales and Delivery Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the DataCo Supply Chain dataset to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, shipping modes, and late delivery risk.

The objective is to turn raw supply chain data into clear business insights using Excel.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard
- Regression Analysis

## Business Questions
1. What are the total sales, total profit, and total number of orders?
2. Which product categories generate the highest sales?
3. Which markets generate the highest sales?
4. What percentage of orders have late delivery risk?
5. Which shipping mode has the highest late delivery risk?
6. Which customer segment generates the most sales?
7. Are higher sales related to higher profit?

## Key Metrics
- Total Orders: 180,519
- Total Sales: $36.78M
- Total Profit: $3.97M
- Average Shipping Time: 3.5 days
- Late Delivery Rate: 55%

## Key Insights
- Fishing was the highest sales category.
- Europe was the top market by sales.
- Consumer was the largest customer segment.
- First Class had the highest late delivery rate.
- Sales and discount were statistically significant variables in the regression analysis.
- Higher sales were positively related to profit, while higher discounts were negatively related to profit.

## Dashboard
The Excel dashboard includes:
- KPI cards
- Top categories by sales
- Sales by market
- Late delivery rate by shipping mode
- Sales by customer segment
- Key insights and recommendations

## Recommendations
- Review First Class shipping performance and delivery promises.
- Prioritize inventory planning for top sales categories such as Fishing.
- Monitor late delivery risk by shipping mode and market.
- Focus service quality on the Consumer customer segment.
- Review discount strategy because higher discounts may reduce profitability.
